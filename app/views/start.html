The Inclusive Design Tool brings together all needs of excluded groups that have been uncovered in UK Health Security Agency research to date to help teams understand the needs of these groups, what inclusion interventions are already in place and identify gaps in research.

We created the tool using many interviews and workshops with designers and researchers and in-depth assessments research findings. It is continuously re-evaluated and updated. This tool should be used early in the design process to iron out obvious barriers. It does not replace diverse research. it must be combined with research and accessibility service standards.

Start now

How to use

As a space to see research, to understand the range and needs of excluded groups. By combining cards to create 'personas' to stress test journeys. This can be done independently or through an Exclusion Mapping workshop with the Inclusive Design team.
